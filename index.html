Long-term memory mode

So you get a good understanding of the classification and the connection of the respective index cards.

If you want to learn long-term and sustainably with a minimum of time and maximum learning success, you should choose this learning method. The Leitner mode automatically presents your learning content at ever increasing intervals. The long-term memory trainer is rounded off by an individually configurable reminder function. It supports you in achieving your learning goal and reminds you of the number of tasks currently to be learned. The power mode was specially developed for short-term exam preparation. He works with a modified Leitner algorithm. In contrast to long-term memory mode, learning content is not drawn according to time intervals, but on the basis of probabilities. Questions that have often been answered correctly are less likely to be drawn than questions that have often been answered incorrectly. So you get the maximum learning success from the last hours before the exam.
